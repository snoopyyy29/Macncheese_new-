The amazing island of Tung Ping Chau is part of UNESCO's Global Geoparks network and is situated in the northeastern region of Hong Kong. Due to its distinct geological makeup, which consists primarily of sedimentary rock, it has a multilayered, colourful environment that is ideal for fossil hunting.
You'll quickly forget about the busy city when you visit the abandoned lead mine in Lin Ma Hang. Lin Ma Hang used to be a restricted region that could only be entered by people with special permits. It is situated in the Frontier Closed region close to Hong Kong's northern border. Eventually, in 2016, restrictions were removed.
