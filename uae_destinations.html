The Moon Island removes you from the hectic pace of the Metropolis. It is situated around 70 kilometres offshore the coast of Dubai.
Surrounded by a charming 3,000-acre oasis, the UAE's "Garden City," abounding in lush foliage, is a perfect destination for a staycation. With 147,000 fruit and date trees, it's a serene area that seems to be totally cut off from the busy city centre that surrounds it.
Its untainted beauty is indisputable, despite being quite Instagram-friendly and a bit touristic during prime hours. Kayak past the stunning, spiky mountains and the turquoise waters of Hatta Dam.
Old brewing pots, coffee grinders from the First World War, and historical information on coffee are just a few of the items on exhibit. The ground floor houses the museum shop, and admission is free
The 300+ kinds of marine animals on display at Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, including sharks and rays, provide an amazing look into the underwater world.
